About Vishwaratna Foundation

Dr. Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar, the architect of the Indian Constitution, is a global symbol of justice, equality, liberty, and fraternity.

After the passing of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar on 6 December 1956, his followers continued his vision. On 28 December 2002, Vishwaratna Foundation was established in Mandwa.

Vishwaratna Foundation is a secular and non-political platform created by common people for the welfare of society.
